About BMS

BMS 2025, held by HMIT ITS, represents a collaborative effort by talented students and professionals passionate about creating innovative digital solutions. Our team, Kelompok 12, participated in the KPP BMS 2025 assignment, where we combined diverse skills in Figma design, portfolio website development, photography/cinematography, and social media content creation to deliver projects that make a meaningful impact.

Through this assignment, we focused on creating visually stunning and functional designs using Figma, developing an engaging and professional portfolio website, capturing high-quality photography and cinematography, and crafting compelling social media content to enhance brand presence. Each member of the team brought their unique perspective and expertise, ensuring that every aspect of the project exceeded expectations.

Our portfolio not only showcases our commitment to excellence but also reflects our dedication to staying at the forefront of digital innovation through this real-world KPP assignment experience.
